Subject: Urgent: Security Problems Detected in Software Development. Dear team, I hope you are well. I want to report a critical security issue we have detected in our software development.

Problem Description: During a recent security review, several vulnerabilities were identified in our system,
including possible SQL injections, lack of input validation in user forms, and unencrypted data transmission.
These vulnerabilities could be exploited by attackers to gain unauthorized access to sensitive data or to
compromise the integrity of our system.
Impact: The discovered vulnerabilities represent a significant risk to the security of our users and the
integrity of our data. At worst, they could result in data breaches that will damage our reputation and incur
significant costs associated with mitigating and recovering from security incidents.
Proposed Solution: I propose the following measures to address the identified problems:
Implement thorough input validations across all user entry points.
Use prepared queries to prevent theft of SQL information.
Secure data transmission using encryption.
Perform a full security audit of the code and apply any necessary fixes.
Establish a regular penetration testing program to identify and resolve future alterations or attempts to steal
information in the software.
I appreciate the immediate attention to this critical issue and am confident that by working together, we can
strengthen the security of our software to protect our users and our organization.
I remain available to coordinate the next steps and provide the necessary support during the implementation of
these measures.
Kind regards.
Tomas Zarate (CEO)
